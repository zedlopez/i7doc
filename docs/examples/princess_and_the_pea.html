Princess and the Pea

Example 173 ★
WI §11.7 Begin and end
RB §8.4 Furniture

The player is unable to sleep on a mattress (or stack of mattresses) because the bottom one has something uncomfortable under it.

172. Matreshka
174. Numberless

The main point here is that we need to figure out where the stack meets the floor:

Play/modify Princess and the Pea in Borogove • Play Princess and the Pea in Quixe

"Princess and the Pea"
  
The Topmost Turret is a room. A mattress is a kind of supporter. A mattress is always enterable. A mattress is portable.
  
A large mattress is a mattress in the Turret. A medium mattress is a mattress in the Turret. A small mattress is a mattress in the Turret.
  
Instead of sleeping when the player is on a mattress (called the bed):
  let the item be the bed;
  while the holder of the item is not a room:
    let the item be the holder of the item;
  say "You can still feel something very uncomfortable under [the item]."
  
Instead of sleeping:
  say "You can't sleep standing up!"
  
Instead of looking under a mattress, say "You scout around, but are unable to determine what's causing you this discomfort. If only your maid Winnie were here. She's very good at this."
  
Test me with "sleep / enter small / sleep / get up / get small / put small on medium / get on small / sleep / get up / g / get medium / put medium on large / get on small / look / sleep".
Test me with "sleep / enter small / sleep / get up / get small / put small on medium / get on small / sleep / get up / g / get medium / put medium on large / get on small / look / sleep".
Topmost Turret
You can see a large mattress, a medium mattress and a small mattress here.

>(Testing.)

>[1] sleep
You can't sleep standing up!

>[2] enter small
You get onto the small mattress.

>[3] sleep
You can still feel something very uncomfortable under the small mattress.

>[4] get up
You get off the small mattress.

Topmost Turret
You can see a large mattress, a medium mattress and a small mattress here.

>[5] get small
Taken.

>[6] put small on medium
You put the small mattress on the medium mattress.

>[7] get on small
(getting onto the medium mattress)
You get onto the small mattress.

>[8] sleep
You can still feel something very uncomfortable under the medium mattress.

>[9] get up
You get off the small mattress.

Topmost Turret (on the medium mattress)
You can see a large mattress here.

On the medium mattress you can see a small mattress.

>[10] g
You get off the medium mattress.

Topmost Turret
You can see a large mattress and a medium mattress (on which is a small mattress) here.

>[11] get medium
Taken.

>[12] put medium on large
You put the medium mattress on the large mattress.

>[13] get on small
(getting onto the large mattress)
(getting onto the medium mattress)
You get onto the small mattress.

>[14] look
Topmost Turret (on the small mattress) (on the medium mattress) (on the large mattress)

>[15] sleep
You can still feel something very uncomfortable under the large mattress.
