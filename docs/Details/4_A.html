Putting something on something (past tense put it on): applying to two things

By this action, an actor puts something he is holding on top of a supporter: for instance, putting an apple on a table.

Typed commands leading to this action

"put [other things] on/onto [something]" - putting it on
"drop [other things] on/onto [something]" - putting it on
